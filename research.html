Research

My research focuses on Bengali NLP and practical ML evaluation. I work with transformer-based models for classification (fake reviews/news, sentiment), study dataset balancing and error patterns, and build deployable pipelines.

1) Bengali Fake Review Detection

Explored transformer-based Bengali review classification by training and comparing multiple pretrained models. Emphasis was placed on dataset balancing, class-wise behavior, and error analysis to understand where models fail and why.

BanglaBERTXLM-REvaluationError Analysis
GitHub Repo

2) Sentiment Analysis of Bengali Movie Reviews

Fine-tuned transformer models for Bengali sentiment classification with a focus on efficient training. Used PEFT-style methods (e.g., LoRA) and compared setups to track accuracy, stability, and generalization across splits.

TransformersLoRA / PEFTPipelineNLP
GitHub Repo

3) Multimodal Fake News Detection

Built a multimodal system combining textual transformers with image features and compared unimodal vs multimodal performance. Used late-fusion to merge signals and analyzed how text+image improves reliability over text-only baselines.
