Research

Fresh-graduate friendly overview

My research interest is in applied Bengali NLP and misinformation detection, focusing on transformer models and LLM-oriented workflows, with practical evaluation and deployment.

1
Misinformation Detection Fake review/news detection using transformer models with balancing, augmentation, and error analysis.
2
Multimodal Learning Text + image fusion strategies and comparison with unimodal baselines.
3
Dataset Preparation SOS dataset (audio + text): labeling strategy, preprocessing, and dataset quality checks.
4
Deployment & Evaluation Backend APIs for inference, confidence-based handling, and consistent preprocessing at inference-time.
