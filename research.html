Research

My research focuses on Bengali NLP, multimodal misinformation detection, and LLM-oriented workflows. I build transformer-based classifiers for fake review/news and sentiment tasks, study class imbalance and error patterns, and develop deployable pipelines that connect models to real-time applications. I’m also exploring Generative AI and reliable evaluation practices for low-resource settings.

1) Bengali Fake Review Detection

Built and evaluated transformer-based Bengali review classifiers by training and comparing multiple pretrained models. The project emphasizes imbalance handling (balancing + augmentation), class-wise behavior, and systematic error analysis to understand where models fail and how to improve reliability.

BanglaBERT XLM-R Evaluation Error Analysis
GitHub Repo

2) Bengali Fake News Detection

Developed a Bengali misinformation detection pipeline with transformer baselines and structured evaluation. Focus areas include robust preprocessing, dataset balancing strategies, and analysis of misclassified samples to identify common linguistic cues and failure cases in real-world news-style text.

NLP Transformers Metrics Pipeline
GitHub Repo

3) Sentiment Analysis of Bengali Movie Reviews

Fine-tuned transformer models for Bengali sentiment classification and compared standard fine-tuning vs PEFT approaches. The work highlights training stability, evaluation consistency, and practical deployment readiness (clean preprocessing and reproducible runs).
