Research Interests

I’m interested in applied Bengali NLP and building reliable ML systems. I focus on practical evaluation, error analysis, and deployment-oriented workflows.

Primary interests

  • Bengali NLP: classification tasks (fake review/news, sentiment) and evaluation for low-resource settings.
  • LLM workflows: fine-tuning strategies, prompt-based baselines, and robust evaluation.
  • Multimodal learning: text + image fusion, comparing unimodal vs multimodal systems.
  • Dataset work: building/cleaning datasets (labeling, preprocessing, quality checks).
Publications: Currently none (will be updated as work gets published).
